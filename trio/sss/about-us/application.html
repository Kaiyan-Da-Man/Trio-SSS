Application and Eligibility

What is the procedure?

Students interested in the program must complete the UNT Student Support Services application, provide income verification and demonstrate academic need. Students who have documented disability must rovide verification from the UNT Office of Disability Access if they are seeking SSS services.

Apply Now

Students who apply to become a part of the TRIO SSS program must be:

  1. A U.S. citizen, national, or permanent resident, as defined below:
    • US Citizen
    • US Natinal (includes natives of American Samoa or Swain's Island)
    • Permanent Resident (U.S. permanent resident who has an I-151, I-551, or I-551C)
    • Eligible Non-Citizen (Arrival-Departure Record (I-94) from the U.S. Immigration and naturalization Service)
    • Not eligible are holders of student visas (F1, F2, J1, and J-2 Visas
  2. Students who are enrolled or accepted for enrollment at UNT as an undergraduate student
