Incentive Requirements

Cultural Enrichment Eligibility Guidelines

In Order to be considered for SSS cultural enrichment activites, an active SSS participant must:

  1. Submit SSS Program Participation Agreement Form
  2. Meet at least 2 times per semester with our SSS Advisor
  3. Complete 1 Financial Literacy Workshop per year
  4. (First-year) students only attending tutory or LEAD Mentoring program
  5. If cumulative GPA falls below 2.25, meet with SSS Advisor or TEAM Advisor to set-up an academic plan

Seniors: Free Gown Rental, SSS Cord, and TRIO Pin Eligibility Guidelines

In order to receive a FREE rental gown from the program, a Senior SSS participant must:

  1. Meet with our SSS Advisor twice before the end of the year.
  2. Submit a resume to your SSS Advisor
  3. Complete SSS Senior Exit Evaluation (here)
